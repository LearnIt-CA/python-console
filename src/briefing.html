Operation Phantom Chase

Briefing: Phantom Hacker

Mission Overview

In February 2025, a hacker known as "Phantom" successfully breached the security systems of Cyberex Exchange, resulting in the theft of cryptocurrency assets valued at approximately $1.5 billion USD.

Our intelligence division has discovered that Phantom communicates with potential buyers through an encrypted channel using a seemingly innocent "Higher or Lower" game as a covert messaging system before executing large transactions.

The CSIS Cyber Division, in collaboration with LearnIT's Python specialists, has recruited you to help intercept and decode these communications.

Your mission is to use Python to reverse-engineer his game system and develop a replacement that will allow us to monitor his communications while appearing identical to his original system.

Target Information

Our intelligence has identified Phantom as a highly skilled individual with a background in game development and cryptography, who works alone to orchestrate complex cyber heists.
