Operation Phantom Chase

Briefing: Phantom Hacker Group

Mission Overview

In February 2025, a hacker collective known as "Phantom" successfully breached the security systems of Cyberex Exchange, resulting in the theft of cryptocurrency assets valued at approximately $1.5 billion USD.

Our intelligence division has discovered that Phantom members communicate through an encrypted channel using a seemingly innocent "Higher or Lower" game as a covert messaging system before executing large transactions.

The FBI Cyber Division, in collaboration with LearnIT's Python specialists, has recruited you to help intercept and decode these communications.

Your mission is to use Python to reverse-engineer their game system and develop a replacement that will allow us to monitor their communications while appearing identical to their original system.

Target Information

Our intelligence has identified the creator of the "Higher or Lower" game system as an individual operating under the alias "ByteMaster". This individual has a background in game development and cryptography.
